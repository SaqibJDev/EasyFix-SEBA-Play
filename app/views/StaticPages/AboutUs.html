About Us

EasyFix is a cost effective smartphone repairing service to help people fixing their broken phones with highly reliable and skilled technician at their doorstep.

We represent an innovative business idea on providing cost-effective smartphone repairing service by offering high-quality tech support services at customers' preferable places i.e. home, office, public place etc.

In addition, we offer repairing companies and self-employed technicians promoting and advertising their services. The aftermath of EasyFix offering and activities is high revenues as it successfully combines brokerage, subscription and advertisement models.
